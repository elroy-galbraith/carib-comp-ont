## Data Processor

**Defined in** §2 of the Data Protection Act 2020.

> "data processor" means, in relation to personal data, any person (other than
> an employee of the data controller) who processes the data on behalf of the
> data controller.

### Notes

- Acts on the **instructions** of the data controller
- Employees of the controller are **excluded** from this definition (they act as agents of the controller)
- Typical processors: cloud service providers, payroll bureaux, third-party analytics firms
- The data controller must ensure processors provide sufficient guarantees about their technical and organisational security measures
