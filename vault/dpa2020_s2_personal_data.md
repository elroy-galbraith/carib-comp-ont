## Personal Data

**Defined in** §2 of the Data Protection Act 2020.

> "personal data" means data which relates to a living individual who can be
> identified from those data, or from those data and other information which is
> in the possession of, or is likely to come into the possession of, the data
> controller, and includes any expression of opinion about the individual and
> any indication of the intentions of the data controller or any other person
> in respect of the individual.

### Key elements

- Relates to a **living individual** (deceased persons excluded)
- Individual must be **identifiable** — directly or by combination with other data held by the controller
- Includes **opinions** about the individual and **stated intentions** of the controller
- Subset **sensitive personal data** (§2) attracts heightened protection
