## Data Subject

**Defined in** §2 of the Data Protection Act 2020.

> "data subject" means an individual who is the subject of personal data.

The data subject is the natural person whose personal data is being processed.
Under the DPA 2020, data subjects hold a suite of enforceable rights including:

- Right of access to their personal data (subject access request)
- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to object to processing
- Right not to be subject to automated decision-making in certain circumstances
