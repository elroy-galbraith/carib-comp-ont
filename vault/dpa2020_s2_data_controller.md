## Data Controller

**Defined in** §2 of the Data Protection Act 2020.

> "data controller" means a person who (either alone or jointly or in common
> with other persons) determines the purposes for which and the manner in which
> any personal data are, or are to be, processed.

### Notes

- Can be a natural or legal person, public authority, or other body
- **Joint controllers** are explicitly recognised ("jointly or in common")
- **Household exemption**: individuals processing data purely for personal/family/household purposes are excluded
- The data controller bears primary compliance obligations under the Act, including registration and adherence to the data protection principles
