## DPA 2020 §2 — Interpretation

Jamaica Data Protection Act 2020, Section 2 — the definitions provision.

> "In this Act, unless the context otherwise requires—"

This section provides the statutory definitions used throughout the Act, including:
**data controller**, **data processor**, **data subject**, and **personal data**.
