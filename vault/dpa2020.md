## Data Protection Act 2020

Jamaica's primary data-protection statute, enacted as **Act No. 5 of 2020**.

**§1 — Citation and commencement**

> This Act may be cited as the Data Protection Act, 2020.

The Act establishes rights for data subjects, obligations for data controllers and
processors, and creates the Office of the Information Commissioner as the
enforcement regulator.
